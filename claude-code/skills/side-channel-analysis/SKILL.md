---
name: side-channel-analysis
description: Use for side-channel attack research, leakage detection, power/EM traces, classifier validation, key recovery, and countermeasure evaluation.
---

Apply side-channel research gates. Check target implementation/binary/firmware/bitstream version, leakage model, acquisition setup, trigger strategy, trace count, alignment/preprocessing, labels/calibration, negative controls, wrong-window controls, leakage tests, classifier validation, ablations, and separation between synthetic leakage, measured leakage, leakage detection, and key recovery. Do not claim real attack success from synthetic-only evidence.
