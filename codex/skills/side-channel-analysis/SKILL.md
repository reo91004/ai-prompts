Apply side-channel research gates. Check target implementation/binary/firmware/bitstream version, leakage model, acquisition setup, trigger strategy, trace count, alignment/preprocessing, labels/calibration, negative controls, wrong-window controls, leakage tests, classifier validation, and ablations. Separate synthetic or simulated evidence, measured leakage detection, isolated-primitive recovery, instrumented-subpath recovery, full-algorithm recovery, and full-KEM recovery. Measured leakage alone never supports a full-path or key-recovery claim. Use `hardware-capture-integrity` whenever physical acquisition is performed.
